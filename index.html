About the Show

Fake Problems Podcast is what happens when two guys with too much free time and not enough impulse control start talking into microphones. Every episode dives into the dark, weird, and absurd corners of life — from near-nuclear disasters to whether penguins could be domesticated.

No guests. No sponsors. No filter. Just two friends who treat conspiracy theories, moral dilemmas, and bizarre hypotheticals with the same deadpan seriousness. If you've ever spent 20 minutes arguing about something that doesn't matter, this is your show.
