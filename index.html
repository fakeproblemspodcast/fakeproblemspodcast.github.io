About

What Is This?

Fake Problems Podcast is what happens when two guys with too much free time and not enough impulse control start talking into microphones.

Every episode dives into the dark, weird, and absurd corners of life with the kind of deadpan confidence that makes you wonder if they're joking. Topics include nuclear accidents, cannibalism, penguin domestication, and whatever else seemed funny at 2 AM.

No guests. No sponsors. No filter.
